About Us

At Giggles we want to uncover your child's true potential and let their imagination grow. Our expertly curated curriculum celebrates your child's curiosity by letting them explore on their own under the guidance of our highly trained teachers. We provide a fun learning experience in the classroom and outdoor learning spaces, gently guiding your child's social, physical, emtional and intellectual growth.

We realise how important your child's safety and education is. We want you to be involved in your child's development and witness their achievements. Thus, we bring your family to the classroom and engage with you in a constant conversation.
